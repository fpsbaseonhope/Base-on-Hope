About Us

We believe students can create meaningful change.

Our mission is to connect students, local communities, and supporters through fundraising projects that are simple, creative, and impactful. We use campaigns, events, and social media to make participation easier and more accessible.

Identity

Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

To create accessible fundraising opportunities where students can contribute to real community impact.

Vision

To become a youth-led platform that connects compassion, creativity, and action.

Values

Hope, responsibility, transparency, teamwork, and consistent action.

Baseball Stadium Fundraising

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Base on Hope?

Any student who wants to support our campaigns, help with fundraising, or contribute to our projects can join.

How can companies support us?

Companies can support us through sponsorships, donations, product support, or collaboration opportunities.

Where will the funds go?

Funds will be used for campaign-related costs and donations to the cause we support. We aim to keep records transparent.
